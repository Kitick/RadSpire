# RadSpire

## World

Spires of radiation began rising from the ground after a massive solar flare, which also knocked out the technology of the society, regressing civilization to the medieval age. Thirty years have passed since.

---

## Entities

### Roland
A knight of the [Order of Astra](#order-of-astra), assigned to investigate rumors of a surviving pre-flare device capable of aiding in the removal of radiation. Despite his doubts about the device's existence, he accepted the mission, which took him beyond the walls of [Lindel](#lindel), the city he had called home since the catastrophe. Early in his travels, he is ambushed by a band of [Warriors of Meldoran](#warriors-of-meldoran), stripped of his weapons and helmet, and left for dead. He survives only by finding shelter and the aid of [Sera](#sera). As he travels further, he finds relics of the past that grant him newfound powers, abilities, and tools.

### Sera
A Wanderer of the [League of Krones](#league-of-krones), operating out of an office building along the road. Her role is reconnaissance, cataloguing the surrounding land, its resources, and its dangers. Practical and capable with her hands, but not a fighter. When Roland stumbles past her building, she recognizes the Meldoran's work and sees an opportunity. Her friend [Dag](#dag) was taken by the same men days earlier and is being held at a ruined gas station nearby. She has been waiting, afraid and unable to act, ever since. She offers Roland shelter, materials, and knowledge drawn from her work in exchange for the one thing she cannot give herself: someone willing to go get Dag back.

### Dag
Sera's friend, captured by [Warriors of Meldoran](#warriors-of-meldoran) and held at a ruined gas station near the road. His current condition is unknown to Sera, which is its own source of dread for her.

### Lindel
The city Roland has lived in ever since the catastrophe. A city under the watch of the [Order of Astra](#order-of-astra).

---

## Factions

### Order of Astra
A protectorate order that formed shortly after the flare. Trades protection for resources with the cities they inhabit. On good terms with the [League of Krones](#league-of-krones), hostile terms with the [Warriors of Meldoran](#warriors-of-meldoran), and neutral with all other factions.

### Warriors of Meldoran
Warrior tribes following a code set forth by their founder, Meldoran. They believe the weak deserve to die and the strong deserve to lead. Generally on hostile terms with all other factions. Also searching for the pre-flare device.

### League of Krones
A society of scavengers and merchants divided into three classes. Scavengers source resources and gear, Merchants sell said resources and gear, and Wanderers do reconnaissance, reporting back areas of interest or areas to avoid. On good terms with the [Order of Astra](#order-of-astra).

---

## The Spire

In the distance from where Roland finds himself, a RadSpire looms. Like all spires, it erupted from the earth after the flare and has been silently bleeding radiation into the surrounding land ever since. Locals who have lived near it long enough know to keep their distance. Those who do not learn quickly. The spire does not pursue, does not threaten, and has no agenda. It simply exists, patient and indifferent, poisoning everything within its reach by degree. The closer one gets, the worse it becomes. It is a reminder that the land itself was wounded by the flare, and that wound has never closed.

---

## Story

The [Order of Astra](#order-of-astra) sends [Roland](#roland) beyond the walls of [Lindel](#lindel) to investigate a rumored pre-flare device capable of removing radiation, a relic that would be world-changing if real. Early in his travels, Roland is ambushed by [Warriors of Meldoran](#warriors-of-meldoran), stripped of his weapons and helmet, and left for dead. He survives by finding shelter nearby, where he meets [Sera](#sera). With her help, Roland recovers and ultimately learns the Warriors are also hunting the device, which hardens his resolve to find it first.

*The current build of the game covers Roland's recovery and the rescue at the gas station. The broader device hunt continues beyond this point.*

## Quests

### Main Quest

#### 1. Left for Dead
**Objective:** Survive the ambush and find shelter.
**Completion:** Reach Sera's building and meet her.
**Progression:** Sera offers to help Roland recover, but only if he helps her first.

**Sera on first meeting:**
> "Hey. Hey, over here. You look like you're about to fall over."
> "Get inside. Those were Meldoran men that did this to you, weren't they? I can tell by the way they left you. They don't finish what they start."
> "I'm not going to hurt you. My name is Sera. I'm a Wanderer with the League of Krones, posted out here for weeks mapping the area. Sit down before you fall down."
> "I know this land better than anyone within ten miles. I know where to find what you'll need. But first — an Astra knight, out here alone? You're not on patrol. What are you looking for?"
> "The neutralizer. I've heard the rumor. The League has heard it too — something pre-flare, something that could pull the radiation back. Nobody knows if it's real or where it is, but people are moving because of it. The Meldoran included, which means you don't have much time to waste out here bleeding."
> "But I have to ask something of you first, and I need you to hear me out."
> "They took my friend. Dag. Same men who did this to you. They've had him for days now and I can't go there myself. I'm not built for it."
> "But you are. Or you were, before they stripped you. Help me get him back, and I'll get you back on your feet. Materials, tools, everything I know. We can talk terms once you've stopped bleeding."

---

#### 2. A Fair Trade
**Objective:** Rescue [Dag](#dag) from [Warriors of Meldoran](#warriors-of-meldoran) holding out at a ruined gas station.
**Completion:** All bandits at the gas station defeated, Dag freed.
**Progression:** End of the current game. Roland is reequipped and has proven himself. The search for the device continues.

**Sera after the deal is struck:**
> "The gas station down the road. That's where they'll have him. There's maybe four or five of them, hard to say. They move around."
> "Before you go anywhere near that place, you are going to need a weapon and something between you and their blades."
> "I don't have everything here, but the land does. Head into the woods for wood and branches, pick up stones along the road, and if you need iron, check the craters. Whatever hit this land left plenty of it behind."
> "Bring what you find back here and I'll show you what to do with it. Then you go get Dag."

**Sera before Roland leaves for the gas station:**
> "That's better. You look less like a corpse now."
> "I can't go with you. I wish I could. Just please be careful."
> "Keep your guard up and don't get greedy. They're thugs, not soldiers, but they'll kill you just the same if you let them."
> "Dag is stubborn and loud and he would have done something stupid trying to get himself out by now. I just hope he's still in one piece. Bring him back."

**Sera after the rescue:**
> "You actually did it. Both of you. I didn't let myself think too hard about what I'd do if you didn't come back."
> "Dag — are you alright? Don't answer that, just sit down."
> **Dag:** "I'm fine. I had it handled."
> "You absolutely did not have it handled."
> **Dag:** "I was working on it."
> "You were sitting in a gas station."
> **Dag:** "Strategically."
> "You did well, Roland. Better than I had any right to hope when I found you bleeding in the road."
> "Your order sent you out here looking for something. I hope you find it. You're not going to find it standing here."
> "Go. And if our paths cross again, the debt goes the other way."

### Side Quests

#### Arm Yourself
**Objective:** Craft equipment with Sera's guidance before heading to the gas station.
- Craft a sword.
- Craft a helmet.
- Craft a shield.

**Completion:** Each item crafted counts as its own objective. None are required to trigger the main quest, but all are recommended before the fight.

**Sera on crafting (sword):**
> "Iron from the craters, wood for the grip. Bring both and we'll put something together."
> "It won't win any prizes, but it'll hold an edge and hold together. That's what matters out here."

**Sera on crafting (helmet):**
> "Stones and iron. There's flat rock along the road and iron in the craters if you dig a little."
> "Head stays on, you keep fighting. Simple as that."

**Sera on crafting (shield):**
> "Wood from the tree line, bound with iron. The woods aren't far, and you already know where the craters are."
> "You're not just trying to hit them. You're trying to not get hit. Keep this between you and whatever's coming at you."

## Opening Excerpt

*Thirty years ago, a solar flare knocked out every machine on earth and split the ground open. Radiation spires rose where nothing had stood before, and civilization collapsed back to the dark ages overnight.*

*You are Roland, a knight of the Order of Astra. Your order has sent you out of the city of Lindel to find a pre-flare device rumored to neutralize radiation. You have your doubts about whether it exists. You set out anyway.*

*You never made it far. A band of Meldoran warriors ambushed you on the road, took your weapons and helmet, and left you for dead. Now you are injured, unarmed, and miles from help.*

*Find shelter. Find help. Get back what was taken.*
